                        # HR Analytics Dashboard 👥📊

A Business Intelligence project in Excel analyzing employee attrition patterns and HR insights.

## 🔧 Tools Used
- Excel
- Charts
- Pivot Table
- HR Dataset

## 📌 Key KPIs
- 👥 **Total Employees:** 1470
- ❌ **Attrition Count:** 237
- ✅ **Remaining Employees:** 1233
- 🎯 **Attrition Rate:** 16%
- ⏳ **Average Age:** 37
- ⭐ **Job Satisfaction:** 2.7


## 🔍 Business Insights

- R&D & Sales show the **highest attrition**.  
- Majority attrition comes from **Lab Technicians and Sales Executives**.  
- **25–34 age group** is most vulnerable to leaving.  
- **Single employees** contribute higher attrition compared to married/divorced.  
- Employees from **Life Sciences & Medical backgrounds** show high turnover.
